## Post Content

Interesting paper.

One conceptual nuance: the proposed measure may be closer to a directional energy imbalance than to volatility in the geometric/path-dependent sense.

It separates signed squared returns, but it does not directly measure path roughness, local fluctuation structure, or movement around a trend.

A possible extension would be to compute the same good-minus-bad energy idea using mean-centered or locally detrended residuals instead of raw returns.
