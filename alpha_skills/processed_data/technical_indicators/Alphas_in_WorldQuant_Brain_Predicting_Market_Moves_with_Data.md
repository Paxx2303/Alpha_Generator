# Alphas in WorldQuant Brain: Predicting Market Moves with Data

## Post Content

In WorldQuant Brain, an **alpha** is a formula designed to predict how a stock or asset might move in the market. It uses historical data such as prices, trading volumes, and market trends to create a signal that can be tested.

Each alpha represents a small, testable idea. The platform evaluates your alpha for accuracy and consistency across multiple market scenarios. Strong and reliable alphas can then be combined with others to create a diversified, data-driven trading strategy.

Building alphas teaches you how to think like a quantitative researcher—analyzing patterns, testing hypotheses, and contributing to real-world financial models.
