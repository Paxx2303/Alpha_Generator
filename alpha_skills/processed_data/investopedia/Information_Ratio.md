# Information Ratio

**Category:** Risk & Performance Metrics 
**Source:** Investopedia / Quantitative Finance Reference

---

## 📝 Definition

Measures active returns generated by a portfolio manager relative to a benchmark per unit of active risk (tracking error).

---

## 🔢 Mathematical Formula

$$\text{IR} = \frac{R_p - R_b}{\text{Tracking Error}}$$

---

## 💡 Application in Alpha Design (WorldQuant Brain)

Evaluates active manager skill. WQ Brain counterpart is the Sharpe ratio of neutralized signals.

- **Neutralization Recommendation:** Typically, price-volume expressions under this concept should use **Market** neutralization to eliminate macro biases, while fundamental expressions can use **Subindustry** neutralization.
- **Tuning Tip:** If the turnover of an alpha built under this concept is too high (e.g. >70%), consider smoothing the final expression with `ts_decay_linear(signal, d)` where `d` is between 3 and 10.
