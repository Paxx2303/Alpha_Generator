# Overview of the Alpha Research Workflow in BRAIN

**Source:** https://support.worldquantbrain.com/hc/en-us/community/posts/37470160043031-Overview-of-the-Alpha-Research-Workflow-in-BRAIN  
**Metadata:** Samuel Njenga waithaka(BO33926) 4 months ago Edited

## Post Content

Alpha research in BRAIN follows a structured workflow. It starts with formulating a hypothesis based on market behavior or data insights. Relevant data is then selected and preprocessed from the data library.

Next, an alpha formula is designed using mathematical and time-series operators and backtested to assess historical performance. The alpha is evaluated using key metrics such as fitness, Sharpe ratio, turnover, and drawdown.

Based on these results, the alpha is refined and improved, validated for robustness across different periods and universes, and finally submitted.
