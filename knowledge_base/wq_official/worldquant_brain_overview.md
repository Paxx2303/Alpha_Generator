# WorldQuant BRAIN Overview

Official source:
- https://www.worldquant.com/brain/
- https://www.worldquant.com/zh-hans/iqc/

Working notes for the local knowledge base:
- WorldQuant BRAIN is a simulation platform for crowdsourced quantitative research.
- Users build alphas from predefined operators and data fields, then evaluate them through platform simulations.
- The platform emphasizes research workflow, performance dashboards, and controlled participation rules.
- Competitions such as IQC are tied to BRAIN participation and have eligibility and rules constraints.
