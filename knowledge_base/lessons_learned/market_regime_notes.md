# Market Regime Notes

- Track whether momentum or reversal signals dominate in the latest approved set.
- Watch turnover drift during high-volatility periods.
- Treat liquidity changes as a regime signal, not just a microstructure detail.
- When drawdown rises, test whether the alpha is implicitly a crowded momentum bet.
- Prefer regime-aware variants of momentum instead of one fixed ranking rule across all states.
- When volume motifs dominate, force exploration of range, volatility, and price-normalization families.
- If turnover spikes, try higher decay or longer lookback before discarding the hypothesis.
- A weaker but lower self-correlation alpha may still be more useful than another crowded winner.
